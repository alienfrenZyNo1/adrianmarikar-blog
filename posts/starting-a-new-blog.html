Starting a new blog

This is the first marker in a new place to publish notes, experiments, and longer thoughts.

The aim is simple: write clearly, keep the site fast, and make it easy to add new posts without turning publishing into a project of its own.

Good personal websites feel lived in. They should be quiet enough to read and flexible enough to change.

Expect essays, build logs, technical notes, and the occasional snapshot of what is currently interesting.
