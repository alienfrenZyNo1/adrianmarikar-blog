A fast personal website

This site is deliberately simple: static files, a small stylesheet, no heavy client-side framework, and an nginx container in production.

That keeps the deployment boring and the reading experience fast. It also makes ownership straightforward: edit a file, commit, push, redeploy.

Good defaults matter. The site includes clean metadata, canonical URLs, an RSS feed, responsive layout, and a visual system that can evolve without a redesign.
